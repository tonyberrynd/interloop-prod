Badges

Use badges as visual indicators for Numberic Values, like notifications, tallies, or other hidden quantities

**For Statuses or other segmented information - use Lozenges


There are three types of badges
  1. Dot Badge: Circular badge that doesn't contain an explicit value
  2. Bold Badge: Dark background with light text
  3. Subtle Badge: Light background with dark text
